Contact

We are located on Tafelbergdreef 148, 3564 AD Utrecht.

Amsterdam

When travelling from Amsterdam you take the A2 and leave at exit 6-8 at Maarssen. You take the N230 and leave on the second roundabout to the Einsteindreef, from there you take the right exit to Oranjerivierdreef and then the first left to Kasaidreef. Then you take a left to the Klipspringdreef and continue this road to come to Tafelbergdreef.

Public transport

If you come with the Public Transportation (OV) you must go to Utrecht Centraal and take Bus 1,3 or 6 with destination Overvecht. You can also take the train to Utrecht Overvecht and take Bus 1 or 11 with destination Overvecht/Leidsche Rijn. In every occasion you get out at Zamenhofdreef and walk away from the shopping mall.
