FAQ

Q: If I book a photoshoot but I can’t make it anymore, is it possible to cancel?

A: It is possible to cancel your appointment 14 hours before the starting time, if you are on a location, it is 12 hours before the photographer leaves. If his time on the road is longer than 12 hours, it is still 14 hours before the start of the shoot.

Q: I want to have a photoshoot in Belgium, is it possible to do this?

A: We travel where you travel in the Netherlands, but if you want your photoshoot in a foreign country you must ask us. In special occasions we are willing to go to a foreign country.

Q: What happens when I don’t like my pictures?

A: We always aim to give you the best things. Our photographer always shows his best picks to you and asks if you want them to be developed. At this point you can dislike the pictures and the photographer sends you a new draft. If you don’t like any of the pictures the photographer will still develop what he thinks are the best pictures and send them to you. If you don’t like the pictures that you got, you can come to our shop and we will help you again.
